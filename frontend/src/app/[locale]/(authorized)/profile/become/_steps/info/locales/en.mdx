## GetTrip - Online service for finding excursions and tours

GetTrip is an innovative platform where travelers can easily find and book tours and excursions from local guides and companies. 
Join our dynamic community of travel enthusiasts, share your knowledge, meet interesting people and earn money doing what you love! 
GetTrip helps you explore new horizons, creating unforgettable experiences for you and your guests.

## Who can become a guide?

We invite both professional guides and amateurs who know their city and are passionate about conducting excursions. 
Join us and share your passion and knowledge to create unique and memorable excursions and tours.
